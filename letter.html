My Little Soda Pop,


People think our story began with a game.


A random match.


A random 1v1.


A random teammate.


A hero selection screen and a “start” button.


But that’s not where it really started.


It started in the quiet moments — when the match ended, but you stayed.


You stayed in the lobby.


You stayed in the conversation.


You stayed when you didn’t have to.


I didn’t choose you because you were good at the game. (hindi ko sinasabi na mahina ka ah)


Not because of win streaks.


Not because you carried me (lakas naman).


I chose you because you noticed.


You noticed when my replies were shorter.


When my jokes weren’t as funny.


When my “I’m fine” wasn’t fine.


You saw me.


Not the version I show everyone — not the confident one, not the serious one, not the one who pretends everything is easy.


You saw the pauses.


The doubts.


The silences between my words.


And you didn’t run.


You stayed.


That’s the part no one else understands.


Out of all the players online, out of all the matches we could’ve queued, you were the only one who didn’t just protect my hero —


you protected my heart.


You chose to see the real me first.


And loving you was the easiest response to that.


Iloveyouuu.


Yuri.
